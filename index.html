ROADMAP

These NFTs have the purpose of creating a different world than the one we
know, but first we have a journey ahead of us.

  • FIRST LAUNCH

    Launching the first NFT Galactic Resistance collection.

  • SURVEY

    Survey’s creation with the community about how they can use their collection whether an NFT game, staking or a marketplace.

  • SPREADING

    Spread the community (marketing).

  • SECOND LAUNCH

    Launching the second collection "The Era begins".

  • LISTING

    Listing of the collection in the marketplaces to be able to exchange them.

  • DAO CHANNEL

    DAO channel for NFT Galactic Resistance holders where they can make decisions and opinions for the project. You will have AMA and prize draws each fortnight.

  • PRIVATE CHANNEL

    Private channel for holders where they will be given access to whitelist, pre-sales to other NFT projects through Galactic Resistance on the Solana network.

  • COLLABORATIONS

    Collabs and partnership with other NFT projects of the same network to expand deals and community between both projects.

  • UNIQUE NFT SALE

    Sale of 500 unique NFT which represents the leaders of the good and dark side of history.

  • DRAWINGS

    After each sale, we will create a draw for all holders and users, you can get phones, computers, and many other things there.

  • NARVICK COLLECTION

    Sale of NFT spaceships collection of the narvick order and the resistance.

  • ROADMAP UPDATE

    The roadmap will be updated depending on which option won in the stage 2 survey.
